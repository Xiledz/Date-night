Closer Tonight is a slow game. The cards aren't trying to entertain you — they're trying to give you a way in.

Whoever draws the card reads it. The other answers. Take your time. The point isn't to get to the next card.

Sometimes a Wildcard will interrupt. They change the rules for one turn. Trust them.

If something hard comes up that needs its own conversation, open the Hard Conversation ritual instead. Eight steps, no timer.

The deck remembers which cards you've drawn so it doesn't repeat. Reset from the menu.
